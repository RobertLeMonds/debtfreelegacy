HOW TO SOW INTO MINISTRY
This ministry was founded to help people - single and married, learn God’s principles for a life of financial freedom, so they can accomplish all that God has called them to do on this earth. Our primary tool is our own Bible-based workbook: “Financial Freedom in 7 Weeks Plus” which is used to train small 7 week Focus Groups in various cities and churches, even in the nation of Fiji. Currently. We hold half-day “Financial Freedom” seminars entitled “Leaving a Financial Legacy” and these events typically encourage people to commit learning the principles of Financial Freedom in 7 Weeks Plus, inspired by God, and to a focus group in many cases. It is common for many focus group graduates to join us as group leaders or leaders-in-training; thus the teaching ministry grows and grows.

Currently all ministry leaders and teachers, as well as our committed Board of Directors, are unpaid volunteers. Our big plans for a new Legacy Edition of the workbook with a companion DVD came to fruition in late 2009. The Legacy Edition is now available through this website or Amazon.com! Our leaders have a real heart to help millions of Spanish-speaking people, by offering the book in Spanish; this was accomplished in 2014. We are committed to never over- spending our actual cash resources... and that is why we boldly encourage you to make tax deductible donations to www.debtfreelegacy.com.

Please prayerfully consider sending a check to partner with us in our campaign to help God’s people get debt free, stay debt free, and learn to sow their resources into the Kingdom of God and the spreading of the salvation message.

We invite your questions by e-mail or phone. Thanks for joining with us to make a difference!
