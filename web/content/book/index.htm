ABOUT THE BOOK
That’s right. With just 7 weeks of study and extra effort you can start breaking free of debt bondage. Learn how to achieve true financial freedom, God’s way. Gain control of your finances now!

The Legacy Edition of Financial Freedom in 7 Weeks Plus is a hands-on, user friendly workbook ideally suited for small Focus Groups sponsored by a local church or ministry. Disciplined readers can also effectively use this for an individual study guide. The new Legacy Edition of the Book even includes a DVD from the authors introducing each chapter’s course material!

You Will Discover How To:

  • Position God smack in the middle of your finances!
  • Break free of debt bondage…and stay free!
  • Use the Bible to lay a firm financial foundation.
  • Develop your own Flexible Spending Plan; which is far superior to a budget.
  • Establish a cash flow control system, so you’ll have more money left at the end of the month, and not month left at the end of the money.
  • Set meaningful goals, including a plan for Biblical Giving plus savings and debt repayment.
  • Recognize and break financial curses and strongholds.
  • Evaluate two simple examples (before & after) of Bob and Mary Jones’ financial forms.
  • Use your own blank forms to build a long-term Family Financial Freedom Plan.
  • Evaluate self-employed income opportunities.
  • Combine: Scripture, Prayer in Agreement and Spiritual Warfare to defeat the real enemy.
  • Pass-on a family legacy of true Financial Freedom.
  • If all this seems too good to be true, please visit the Testimonies and Endorsements section of this website.
  • Pastors & Ministry Leaders: Now – One Workbook provides a complete Financial Study Guide. Ideal for small Focus Group Leaders and Students.
